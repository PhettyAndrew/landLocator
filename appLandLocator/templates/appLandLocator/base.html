About and Contact Us

The project aims at easening location of viable land for agricultural purposes.
The system (project) acts as a middleman to enable the user (interested party) to access the land for purchasing or leasing purposes.
The project is a solution to challenges such as several pieces of viable land not being used because of difficulty in accessing the land due to remoteness and lack of knowledge about the existence of the land.It also eliminates human middlemen in acquisition of land for leasing or purchase purposes hence reliability in the provided information about the land and acquisition agreements.
